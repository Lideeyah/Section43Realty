About Section43Realty

Section43Realty is on a mission to make land ownership in Nigeria transparent, safe, and accessible for everyone. Inspired by Section 43 of the Nigerian Constitution; which guarantees every citizen the right to own property anywhere in the country; we are building a new kind of real estate brand.

A brand that stands for trust, simplicity, and community empowerment, not hidden fees, fake documents, or confusion. Whether you're buying your first plot or securing land for your family's future, Section43Realty is here to make the process clear and reliable; every step of the way.
